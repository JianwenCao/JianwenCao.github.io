At **UZH RPG**, I focused on Event Camera-Enhanced Robot Motor Control Policies.
- Conducted autoregressive pre-training on multimodal data.
- Significantly improved the performance and robustness of downstream vision and control tasks.
- Leveraged the high temporal resolution of event cameras for robot control.
